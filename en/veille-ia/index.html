How it works

An AI agent scans the reference accounts and feeds every day (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Mistral, Meta, xAI…), removes duplicates and noise, and writes a neutral summary. The same content goes out as a Telegram digest and feeds this archive. Days with no notable announcements stay empty — no filler.
