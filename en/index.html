An AI agent that processes supplier invoices — in production, on its own.

It reads emails every 15 min, extracts each invoice, matches it to the right Odoo purchase order, blocks price discrepancies > 5%, and pushes it for approval in Teams. Running on a Scaleway instance at ~€36/month. The real win isn't speed: it's a workflow that can no longer drift — where manual entry had let ~€2M in off-chain invoices slip through.

How I use Claude

Read the full guide →

The core of my method comes down to a handful of .md files I paste into Claude's "Project Instructions." A main CLAUDE.md that defines my tone, my rules, and my context; extension files loaded on demand (strategy, dev…); and a dedicated file for the DNA of each brand.

The guiding principle: Compound Engineering. Every time Claude gets my style wrong, I add a rule. The system improves with use — and all future work benefits from it.
