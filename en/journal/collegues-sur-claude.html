Colleagues start using Claude

Claude · Cobra · 2025–2026 · Cobra
metaen-cours

What's happening

Claude isn't just a solo tool anymore. Colleagues at Cobra are starting to use it — first for one-off questions, then for more structured tasks.

The challenges

  • How do you share context without sharing credentials?
  • How do you make sure everyone's using the right CLAUDE.md?
  • How do you stop people reinventing the wheel on topics that are already documented?

What's in place (or in progress)

  • CLAUDE.md per project with rules and context
  • Separation of work account vs personal account
  • Knowledge Hub as a shared reference: colleagues read the documented patterns here
