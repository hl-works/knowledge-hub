About

Hugo
Lahutte

In love with audio, image and anything that stirs emotion. 17 years growing Son-Vidéo.com, today CEO of Cobra, publisher of Haute Fidélité and founder of HL Consulting — and I now build nearly all of my tools and content with Claude.

My background

It all starts with a passion: music and cinema. As a student, I sold electronics at Darty, then dipped into events and Japanese culture by helping organize Japan Expo for five years — stage manager of the main hall, logistics, sound. You learn to adapt to anything there.

Then came the defining adventure: 16 years at Son-Vidéo.com, France's number-one online hi-fi and home-cinema retailer. I touched everything — purchasing, sales, commercial strategy, marketing, advertising (on and offline), brand creation and import. Head of purchasing, then of marketing communications, then of commercial strategy, then of partnerships. That's where I learned how to take a company from €10M to €62M, and where I built a network across the whole industry.

What keeps me going

An unapologetic geek, in love with music and cinema, drawn to design and beautiful objects — my desk looks like my interests (Star Wars, Dune, vinyl, gorgeous speakers…). That world feeds directly into HiFi Lovers.

What drives me today is passing it on: showing how AI concretely changes a team's day-to-day, and helping as many people as possible get their hands on it. That's the whole point of this Knowledge Hub and of my method.
